Privacy Policy

At El Arte del Código we are committed to protecting our users' privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share the information we gather.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information when people use our services. This may include, among other data, names, email addresses, IP addresses, and approximate location.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect to deliver and improve our services, personalize the experience, and stay in touch with users. We may also rely on it for research and analysis.

3. How We Share the Information

We do not share our users' personal information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • When the user has given us consent to share the information
  • When required by law or a court order
  • When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety

4. How We Protect the Information

We take reasonable security measures to protect any information we collect. Even so, no system can be guaranteed as completely secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make meaningful changes, we will let you know by email or with a notice on the site. Please review this document regularly to stay informed.

This Privacy Policy has been in effect since May 1, 2023.
