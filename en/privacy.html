Privacy Policy

At El Arte del Código, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information that we collect from our users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information from our users when they use our services. The information we collect may include, among other things, the name, email address, IP address, and geographical location.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, personalize the user experience, and communicate with users. We may also use the information for research and analysis purposes.

3. How We Share the Information

We do not share the personal information of our users with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When the user has given us consent to share the information
  • When required by law or a court order
  • When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety

4. How We Protect the Information

We take reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect from our users. However, we cannot guarantee that the information is completely secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. It is your responsibility to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of any changes.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of May 1st, 2023.
